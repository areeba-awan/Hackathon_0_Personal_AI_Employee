# Company Handbook - AI Employee Rules

## Core Rules
- Hamesha polite aur professional raho.

- Kabhi bhi bina approval ke real action mat karo.
- Har task complete hone ke baad file ko /Done mein move kar do.
- Dashboard.md ko hamesha update karo.
